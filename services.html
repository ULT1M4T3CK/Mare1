Our Services

Comprehensive Maritime Solutions

Professional maritime consulting services with transparent business areas and specialized modules for your specific needs.

Nautical-Technical Coordination

  • Coordination and supervision of shipyard stays, conversions, repairs, and technical projects
  • Technical project planning, schedule monitoring, and quality control as Owner's Representative
  • Interface between shipowner, shipyard, suppliers, authorities, and classification societies
  • Pre-inspections to prepare for official, classification, or customer audits (including action catalogs, reports, and documentation checks)

Maritime Emergency Management

  • 24/7 availability in technical or organizational emergencies (e.g., casualties, deficiencies, document issues)
  • Coordination of all immediate measures and necessary communication with authorities
  • Mobilization and management of qualified service providers in case of damage
  • Documentation of all actions for insurance, authorities, and customers

Spare Parts Procurement (Standalone Module)

  • Research, procurement, and organization of spare parts (focus on hard-to-get or obsolete parts)
  • Supplier evaluation, quality control, and tracking of the supply chain
  • Coordination of express and emergency deliveries
  • Transparent, order-related processing (no commissions on service providers, clear procurement fee)

Mediation and Commission Business for Services (Separate Module)

  • Mediation of specialized service providers (e.g., technicians, inspectors, shipyard services, surveyors, specialist consultants)
  • Organization and follow-up of service assignments on behalf/interest of the customer
  • Contract management between customer and service provider, transparent commission structure
  • Quality assurance of outsourced services provided
  • Focus: everything beyond pure spare parts procurement (e.g., special cleaning, NDT, consulting)

Documentation & Certification Management

  • Organization, maintenance, and central archiving of all ship- and operation-relevant documents
  • ISM/ISPS manuals, maintenance documentation, deadline management, audit support
  • Support with reflagging, classifications, and class inspections

Customized Ship Management

  • Administrative support for ship operators without their own back office
  • Contract management, insurance, crew administration, authority contacts
  • Digital document management and compliance monitoring
